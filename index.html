About Me

I am a passionate and creative UI/UX designer, committed to delivering exceptional and user-centric design solutions. My design approach is grounded in a deep understanding of user behavior and a keen eye for aesthetics. As a recent graduate with a degree in B.E(CSE), I have acquired a solid foundation in design principles, user experience research, and prototyping.

During my academic journey, I actively participated in various design projects, allowing me to refine my skills in creating intuitive and visually appealing user interfaces. I thrive on turning complex problems into simple, elegant, and effective design solutions. My goal is to leverage my knowledge and creativity to craft meaningful and user-centered experiences that leave a lasting impact.

In addition to my academic achievements, I have a strong proficiency in industry-standard design tools such as Adobe XD, Sketch, and Figma. I am also well-versed in conducting user interviews, usability testing, and creating wireframes and prototypes. My dedication to staying updated with the latest design trends and technologies allows me to bring fresh and innovative ideas to every project.
